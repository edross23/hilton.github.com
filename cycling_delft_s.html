Where to go next - options

  • Continue 15 Km NNW via the centre of the Hague to the coast at Scheveningen, and cycle NE up the coast towards Katwijk.
  • Cycle 15 Km WNW via Wateringen to the coast at Kijkduin, and cycle SW down the coast towards Hook of Holland.
  • Cycle back to Rotterdam on the South route along the Overschie canal.
  • Cycle 20 Km SW via Schipluiden and Maasland to Maassluis.
